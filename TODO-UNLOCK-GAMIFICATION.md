

🏆 WORLD-CLASS GAME DEVELOPER RECOMMENDATIONS
1. 🎯 RETENTION OPTIMIZATION
Current Strengths: Streak system, daily quests, login rewards, achievements
Recommendations:

Feature	Current State	Recommended Improvement
Streak Protection	Costs 50 credits to recover	Add "Streak Freeze" power-up purchasable with XP (not credits)
Daily Quests	Static 4 quests	Implement Weekly Boss Quests - harder quests that reset weekly with bigger rewards
Achievements	Only 15, mostly static	Add Dynamic Milestones (e.g., "Answer 1000th question", "First 100% score")
Login Rewards	7-day cycle	Add Monthly Mega Reward on day 30 with exclusive avatar/badge
Implementation Priority: HIGH - These directly impact DAU/MAU metrics

2. 💰 ECONOMY BALANCING
Current Issues Observed:

Credit earning vs spending is heavily skewed toward spending
AI unlock costs 10 credits but AD only gives 5 credits (+30 XP)
No "credit sink" for long-term players
Recommended Credit Sinks:

Streak Shield - Pay 25 credits to protect streak for 1 day
Rank Boost - Pay credits to get bonus XP toward next rank (1.5x cost)
Premium Questions - Pay 3 credits for "expert-level" questions
Name Glow - Pay credits for custom username colors/effects
Squad Boost - Pay credits to give XP bonus to squad members
